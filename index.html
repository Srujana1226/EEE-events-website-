ABOUT US

We The students of the Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) Department are proud to celebrate the birth anniversary of the father of electrical engineering, Michael Faraday, with a day of innovation and discovery. On this momentous occasion, we come together to honor Faraday's groundbreaking contributions to the field, which have paved the way for countless technological advancements. Through interactive exhibits, experiments, and presentations, we will delve into the fascinating world of electromagnetism and explore the impact of Faraday's work on modern society. Join us as we commemorate the legacy of this trailblazing scientist and inspire a new generation of innovators to follow in his footsteps."
